About Us

Empower Your Projects with Expert Engineering Support – On Your Terms

At our company, we understand that finding the right engineering talent for your project can be challenging, especially when you don't need full-time support. That’s where we step in. With extensive experience from some of Sweden's most technologically advanced companies like Saab and ES-range, we offer part-time access to top experts in embedded systems, automation, and electrical engineering.

Whether you need full project execution from concept to completion or just support with specific phases, our services are flexible to match your needs. We can help you develop a new product from idea to prototype and even support mass production—or simply assist with certain tasks in your ongoing project.

What Sets Us Apart:

Highly Skilled Expertise: With our background in advanced system development and niche technologies like radar and space systems, we deliver solutions that drive innovation and efficiency.

Comprehensive or Targeted Support: Need end-to-end project development? We’ve got you covered—from initial design to a working prototype, all the way to mass production. Or perhaps you just need help solving a specific technical challenge or updating a part of your system? We're ready to jump in wherever you need us most.

Cost-Effective, Part-Time Services: Get access to top engineering talent without the commitment and expense of full-time hires. Our flexible, part-time model allows you to get the expert support you need only when and as you need it, making your development process more efficient and affordable.

Flexibility & Speed: Our team is available during afternoons, evenings, and weekends, offering faster turnaround times for your short-term or urgent projects without compromising quality.

By partnering with us, you get the unique advantage of integrating highly qualified professionals into your team seamlessly, whether for a small boost on a project or managing the entire development process—all without the need for a full-time commitment.
